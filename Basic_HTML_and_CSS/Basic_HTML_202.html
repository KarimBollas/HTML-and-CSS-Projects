Physics quantities

Scalar quantities

Scalar quantities only have magnitude (size).
For example, 11 m and 15 ms are both scalar quantities.
Scalar quantities change when their magnitude changes.

Vector quantities

Vector quantities have both magnitude and magnitude direction.
For example, 11 m east and 15 m at 30Â° to the horizontal are both vector quantities.
Vector quantities change when: their magnitude changes; their direction changes; or their magnitude and direction both change.
